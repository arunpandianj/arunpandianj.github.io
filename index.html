About Me

I am an Assistant Professor Senior Grade–I at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore. My research interests include Computer Vision, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Precision Agriculture, Healthcare, and AI Infrastructure & Operations. I completed my Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Anna University (January 2021) and have published several journal and conference papers, filed patents, and conducted workshops on deep learning and NVIDIA platforms.
